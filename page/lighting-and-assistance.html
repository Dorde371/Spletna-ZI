Lighting and assistance systems
We can't see into the future. But we're close.
Lights.
The headlights of 911 models are fitted with cutting-edge LED technology for fast responsiveness and powerful illumination. Matrix technology enables the system to control dynamic segments of the light beam. A camera detects oncoming traffic and adjusts the light pattern to avoid dazzling other road users. Side spotlights provide extra illumination at critical angles. The system also assists in bends and the taillights are also LED.

Assistance systems.
Porsche WET mode
Sensors in the front wheel housing detect wet road surfaces and warn the driver. The system can automatically adjust the responsiveness of the drive and chassis systems for greater safety.
Warn and Brake Assist
Warn and Brake Assist can help reduce the severity of collisions or even avoid them entirely by warning the driver and, if necessary, automatically applying the brakes.
Lane Keep Assist (LKA) with Traffic Sign Recognition
Helps keep your vehicle in lane, recognizes traffic signs, and displays them in the instrument cluster. The system can also warn you of speed limits and overtaking restrictions.
Lane Change Assist (LCA)
LCA monitors the area behind and to the side of your vehicle and warns you of vehicles in your blind spot. The system is active at speeds above 30 mph and can help make lane changes safer.
Night Vision Assist
Night Vision Assist uses an infrared camera to detect pedestrians and large wildlife before they are illuminated by the headlights, and displays a thermal image in the instrument cluster.
ParkAssist (front and rear) and reversing camera
ParkAssist provides visual and audible alerts while parking. The reversing camera displays the area behind the vehicle on the central display, making parking and maneuvering easier and safer.
