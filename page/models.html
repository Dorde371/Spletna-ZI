Highlights.
Models
2020 911 Carrera

The quintessential Porsche coupe with 379 hp twin-turbo boxer 6, 0–60 mph in 4.0s (3.8s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 182 mph. Pure rear-wheel drive performance in a timeless package.

2020 911 Carrera Cabriolet

Open-top driving with 379 hp twin-turbo power, 0–60 mph in 4.2s (4.0s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 180 mph. Experience the wind in your hair with Porsche's signature performance.

2020 911 Carrera S

Enhanced performance with 443 hp, 0–60 mph in 3.5s (3.3s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 191 mph. Upgraded brakes and optional 7-speed manual for the ultimate driving experience.

2020 911 Carrera S Cabriolet

Convertible performance with 443 hp, 0–60 mph in 3.7s (3.5s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 190 mph. The perfect blend of open-air freedom and high-performance capabilities.

2020 911 Carrera 4

All-wheel drive confidence with 379 hp, 0–60 mph in 4.0s (3.8s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 180 mph. Exceptional traction and handling in all conditions.

911 Carrera 4 Cabriolet

All-weather convertible with 379 hp, 0–60 mph in 4.2s (4.0s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 179 mph. Open-top driving with all-wheel drive security.

911 Carrera 4S

Ultimate all-wheel drive performance with 443 hp, 0–60 mph in 3.4s (3.2s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 190 mph. Enhanced brakes and traction for maximum control.

911 Carrera 4S Cabriolet

Top-tier convertible performance with 443 hp, 0–60 mph in 3.6s (3.4s with Sport Chrono), and a top speed of 188 mph. All-wheel drive security meets open-air exhilaration.
