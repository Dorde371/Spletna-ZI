Comfort and infotainment

Roof.

Only a single line is required to describe the 911 design, which has remained unique for decades. That iconic shape is reflected in what is arguably our greatest innovation: the retractable roof system. The 911 Cabriolet's roof is a technical masterpiece, and its contemporary counterpart in the current 911 is no exception.

To appreciate the design of the 911, it's best to experience the design of the 911 Cabriolet. Its roof is lightweight and can be opened and closed at speeds of up to 31 mph. The permanently tensioned rear window and the panel bow-shaped magnesium elements make the roof highly robust and weatherproof, even at high speeds. For a feeling of open-top driving, the roof can be partially opened and closed, keeping the cabin as quiet as possible.

The 911 Cabriolet models also feature an electrically powered wind deflector, offering even greater enjoyment when driving with the top down and minimal wind noise.

Interior.

Sport Steering Wheel

Fitted as standard, the multifunction Sport Steering Wheel with gearshift design is lightweight and functional. It allows for easy access to audio, phone, and navigation functions.

The Sport Steering Wheel has manual fore/aft and height adjustment as well as two gearshift paddles. The two small thumb wheels provide rapid and precise fine adjustment, making cockpit rapid and direct response and the Porsche Crest add to its sporty character.

GT Sport Steering Wheel

With a thumb rest and 12 o'clock marking, as well as easy-grip steering wheel, unique design, the GT Sport Steering Wheel features a clear and direct connection to the road and the car. Distinctive design surrounding the Porsche Crest. The back of the steering wheel is clad in fine leather, and the Sport Response button is standard. The GT Sport Steering Wheel is also available with Accent stitching.

Sound System.

Sound Package Plus

Thanks to 8 loudspeakers and a total output of 150 watts, Sound Package Plus delivers excellent audio quality for your new 911. The amplifier is integrated and speakers are optimally positioned in the interior and passenger side to deliver impressive sound for PCM.

BOSE® Surround Sound System

The BOSE® Surround Sound System is fitted as standard. The 12 speaker system including a subwoofer and 100 watt active subwoofer, features a patented signal processing and the audio volume is automatically adjusted to the level of background noise. Total output is 570 watts. The BOSE® Surround Sound System is an extremely well-matched system, transforming the car into a concert hall. BOSE® Surround Sound System delivers impressive sound quality and dynamics for compressed file formats.

Seats.

Sport Seats

The Sport Seats are not only comfortable—they also provide secure support during dynamic cornering. They are equipped with electric seat height and backrest adjustment as well as a manual fore/aft adjustment for the driver and front passenger. A sporty seat position is ensured by the low seat height and the firm side bolsters. The two-way Sport Seats with memory package provide even greater comfort. Leather support, for example, can be adjusted electrically, as can the seat cushion and backrest angle. A memory function for driver and front passenger seats and the exterior and interior mirror adjustment are also included.

Sport Seats Plus.

Sport Seats Plus offer firmer side bolsters, electric height and backrest adjustment, and a memory function for comfort and support.

Seat Heating and Seat Ventilation.

Standard seat heating and optional ventilation ensure comfort in any weather.
