Exterior.
Okay, a single word is enough to describe the 911 design: iconic. But that would be too little to do justice to the 911. The shape: timeless. The design: unmistakable. The technology: always one step ahead. The result: the most beautiful and modern version of the 911.
The essence of the design of the 911, from the sloping roofline to the pronounced shoulders, is instantly recognizable. Every detail, from the seamless light strip at the rear to the flush door handles, is crafted for both form and function. The wide rear and muscular stance emphasize performance, while the refined lines and surfaces highlight elegance and innovation.

Viewed from the front, the 911 has the aura of a thoroughbred sports car. The wide, low stance provides presence and poise. The cockpit, with its clear lines and advanced technology, is designed for the driver. Every control is exactly where you expect it to be, making the driving experience intuitive and exhilarating.

The new instrument panel of the 911 is inspired by the original 911, blending the classic five-dial layout with modern digital displays. The result is a harmonious fusion of tradition and innovation.

Interior.

Comfort and sportiness, the positioning low, like the original 911. The interior is a place for drivers, with every detail designed to enhance the driving experience. Premium materials, precise craftsmanship, and advanced technology create an environment that is both luxurious and functional.

The horizontal arrangement of the controls is striking, emphasizing the width of the interior. The center console rises towards the dashboard, putting all key functions within easy reach. The seats offer exceptional support, whether on a spirited drive or a long journey.

The instrument cluster with the analog tachometer is flanked by two high-resolution displays, providing all the information you need at a glance. The Porsche Communication Management (PCM) system is seamlessly integrated, offering intuitive control of navigation, media, and connectivity features.

The technical function, alongside all-embracing comfort, is at the heart of the 911 interior. Every surface, every switch, and every seam is a testament to Porsche's commitment to quality and innovation.
