Drive and chassis
Making the seemingly impossible, possible.
Engine.
The twin-turbo engine of the 911 models provides exceptional power, direct responsiveness, and a wide torque plateau—even at low speeds. Performance responds instantly to the driver.
Turbochargers.
Each engine has two turbochargers and an intercooler. The repositioned intercoolers under the rear lid grille improve cooling and power delivery.
911 Carrera & 911 Carrera 4 models.
3.0-liter 6-cylinder twin-turbo, 379 hp, 331 lb-ft torque from 1,950 rpm. 0–60 mph in 3.8s (Sport Chrono), top speed 182 mph.
911 Carrera S & 4S models.
3.0-liter 6-cylinder twin-turbo, 443 hp, 390 lb-ft torque. 0–60 mph in 3.2s (Sport Chrono), top speed 190 mph.
Transmission.

7-speed Manual Transmission
For those who love the classic sports car feel, the 7-speed manual transmission offers precise gear changes and a direct connection to the road.

8-speed Porsche Doppelkupplung (PDK)
The advanced 8-speed PDK offers ultra-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery, perfect for both everyday driving and the racetrack.

Chassis.

Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM)
Electronically adjusts the damping force for each wheel based on current road conditions and driving style.

Porsche Stability Management (PSM)
Enhances stability and traction, especially in challenging driving situations.

Rear axle steering

Agility & Stability
Improves agility at low speeds and stability at high speeds by turning the rear wheels in the same or opposite direction as the front wheels.

Front axle lift system

Raises the front of the car to avoid scraping on ramps or driveways.

Porsche Dynamic Chassis Control (PDCC)

Reduces body roll and stabilizes the car during dynamic cornering.

Drive modes.

Normal, Sport, Sport Plus, Individual
Choose from a range of driving modes to suit your mood and the road ahead.

Sport Chrono Package
Includes a mode switch and a digital/analog stopwatch for even more dynamic performance.

Wheels.

Choose from a variety of wheel designs, each engineered for performance and style. Lightweight alloys, striking finishes, and a range of sizes let you personalize your 911.

Brakes.

The braking system of the 911 is tailored and optimized for maximum performance, with large diameter discs and advanced cooling for fade-free stopping power.

Porsche Ceramic Composite Brakes (PCCB)
Optional PCCB offers even greater resistance to fade and lighter weight for track use.
