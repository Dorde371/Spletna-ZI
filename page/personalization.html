The 911 Carrera 4S in Chalk.

A subtle statement: Chalk. Complementing the distinctive color, this 911 Carrera 4S features SportDesign wheels and trim elements painted in high-gloss black. The interior combines Slate Grey with Lizard Green accents—including the stitching of the 'PORSCHE' logo. Stainless steel pedals and footrest add a final touch.

Contemporary appeal. Subtle in tone. Pure.

The 911 Carrera S Cabriolet in Aventurine Green Metallic.

A color born from nature meets sophisticated engineering. The 911 Carrera S Cabriolet in Aventurine Green Metallic features Exclusive Design wheels accented in exterior color. Inside, Black leather with Truffle Brown stitching complements Aventurine Green Metallic elements—while the interior package in carbon adds a contemporary edge. These thoughtful touches create a cohesive design bringing the natural world into this performance masterpiece.
